Equipment

The equipment used will effect the quality and quantity of the plant.

Grow Area

A grow tent is important as it allows you to create a sealed space where you can regulate humidity, temperature, CO2 levels, and light.

Soil

The quality of soil used will effect both quality and quantity of your yield.

Nutrients

Nutrients are vital to the health of a plant. Just like humans, plants require additional nutrients that they cannot produce themselves. Plants require specific nutrients depending on what stage they are in (Sprout, vegetative, and flowering)

Lighting

Lighting is another vital need of plants. There are various different lighting technologies to choose from. You must also take into account power consumption as you will be running the light near 24/7

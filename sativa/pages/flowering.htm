Flowering

The flowering stage of Cannabis Sativa is where the main psychoactive chemical, delta-9 Tetrahydrocannabinol (THC), is produced. As the plant matures throughout the flowering stage, the small hairs will turn brown. while the trichomes turn from milky white to an amber color.

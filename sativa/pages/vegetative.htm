Vegetative

The vegetative stage of Cannabis Sativa is where the plant grows its many leaves and branches. This is when you manipulate the branches to change how the plant grows. Plants can be grown in many different shapes; From wide, tall, short, and bushy. Some strains of the plant can grow up to 13ft tall! When shaping the plant, keep in mind that the flowers will form at each node. Damaging the nodes of the plant will affect the flowers produced.



Stress Producing Factors to consider

  • High wind speed

  • Soil pH (acidity)

  • Too much/lack of light

  • Under/over watering

  • Nutrient levels
